About Satta Matka

Satta Matka is an old and popular lottery game that became highly popular in the 1950s and still today is sought after by millions of players.

The original purpose of the game was to place bets on the opening and closing rates of cotton that was traded on the New York and Bombay cotton exchange.

However, Satta Matka has since evolved many times. From cotton being replaced to other commodities, to playing cards representing the winning results.

Today, traditional Satta Matka is outlawed in India. However, many variations of the game can still be played legally online, including all of those that we offer here at JungliWIN.
